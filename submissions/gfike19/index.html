The World of Argos

Eons ago, in a galaxy far far away there was a world filled with magic. Elves, humans, dwarves, and every sort of magical beast imaginable lived in harmony. Then a human named Thrakos was spurned by the love of his life for she loved an elf named Aseph who was more powerful then he. Thrakos became enraged and sought to become a magician, using dark and forbidden arts. His magic taxed the land, for with magic the energy must come from either the user or elsewhere. His love was killed by such means and yet he still did not blame himself for these action. Rahter he used his magic to enslave the elves and all who sought to aid them.

Dismayed by the loss of life around them, a coalition of creatures from dragons to centaurs formed and attempted to overthrow Thrakos but to no avail for he empowered those who had held prejudices against all who were non humans. In time the land was sapped dry of its energy and source and so the mad magician moved west toward the mountains to find more stores of energy to maintain his powers

Though Thrakos had left and the land eventually healed thanks to the diligent work of the elves, mistrust among the races still lingered. No longer was there union amongst one another, humans feared magic lest another Thrakos arise. Dwarves went deep into the mountains for the outer world had decimated their numbers. The merfolk fled to the farthest of the seas. The creatures of the forest from fauns to pixies hid from all. Anyone not like themselves was not to be trusted. This is where this story begins
